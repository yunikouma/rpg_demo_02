Overview

Raining Chain is a video game about a video game. Lord Dotex, the leader of the VIRUS gang, managed to access the game source code. Your mission is to fix all the glitches he had caused and save the NPCs from his evil plans. Those NPCs are aware that they are part of a video game and they worship two divinities: The Creator and The RNG God.

The Creator

The Creator has the full control over the game code and data. He forged the maps one by one and gave life to every NPC in the game. He has the power to create and destroy anything. He often materializes in the game as the player "rc" to greet newcomers and check the game state. Using His special tools, He has the power to alter the game state while it's running.

The Creator

The RNG God

The Random Number Generator God is the one who decides everything related with randomness. This includes but is not limited to enemy behaviour, stats on new weapons and damage dealt. Not much is known about how his decision-making works. His secret lies deep within the core engine, under the function Math.random().
