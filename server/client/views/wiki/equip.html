Equip

There are 5 different equipment slots (Weapon, Body, Helm, Amulet and Ring). Each of them come in different types increasing their power toward a certain element.
You can obtain equipment by killing monsters, completing quests and trading with other players.

Upgrading Equipment

Equipment can be upgraded in two ways.
First, you can spend exp to upgrade your equip and increase its Damage/Defence by 5%.
Secondly, you can use materials to unlock boosts that will make you more powerful.



The amount of boosts on an equip is randomly-generated.

Boost Amount Color Chance
0 White 0%
1 White 0%
2 Blue 57%
3 Blue 31.9%
