Experience

Exp can be used to improve your equipment or to level up.
Levelling up grants a Reputation Point which improves a stat of your choice.
Experience is obtained by killing monsters, completing quests and finishing in Top 5 of the Weekly Competitions.

Note: You start off with 10,000 exp. The exact formula is
  Exp = 10000 * 10 ^ (Level / 25)

Global Experience Modifier

Every amount of exp gained is multiplied by your Global Experience Modifier (GEM).
Your GEM starts off at x1 and increases as you complete quests and challenges.

Completing a quest increase your Quest Score for that quest. That Quest Score is used to determined the bonus to your GEM.
Beating a challenge also increase your GEM by +0.02.
The current maximum GEM is x2.90 for completing all 19 quests and 54 challenges, which grants x2.9 more exp than usual.
